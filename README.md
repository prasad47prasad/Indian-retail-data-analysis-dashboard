# 🛒 Indian Retail Data Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analysing Indian supermarket retail data using Power BI to uncover sales trends, profit insights, and customer behaviour.

The dataset contains 40,000+ records, simulating real-world retail operations across multiple cities and regions.

---

## Objectives
- Analyse total sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand regional and city-wise performance
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends

---

##  Dataset Details
- Total Records: 40,000+
- Columns: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Category, City, Region, Date, Customer Feedback, etc.
- Data Type: Simulated Indian supermarket data

---

##  Dashboard Pages

### 1️ Overview
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders & Quantity
- Top 3 Profitable Products
- Monthly Sales Trend

### 2️ Demographics
- Region-wise Sales
- Category-wise Discount Analysis
- Customer Feedback Distribution

### 3️ Analysis
- Total Sales by Category
- Monthly Sales Performance
- Trend Analysis

### 4️ Profit Insights
- Gross Profit
- Monthly Profit Comparison
- Region-wise Profit by City

### 5️ Details
- Transaction-level data
- Filters for City, Category, Feedback, Date

---

##  Key Insights
- Certain regions contribute significantly to overall revenue
- Discounts have a direct impact on profit margins
- Specific product categories consistently generate higher profits
- Seasonal trends affect sales performance

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

##  Features
